Steps to follow

  1. Enter the voters full name using the order below.

    surname firstname middlename

  2. Enter your date of birth

    dd/mm/yr

  3. Enter your email address, if you don't have an email address you can skip the step.

  4. Create a strong password which will be easy enough for you to remember.

    it must include a Capital letter,a number and a symbol.

  5. Re-enter the same password you put before and confirm to make sure its similar to the one put above it.

  6. Enter your voter registration ID

  7. Click on the submit button
